About us

As early as 2019 LNK Africa started as a IT sales services and solutions provider in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

The quality of the services offered to businesses have been able to cover the shortcomings on the market thanks to the significant experience of the team which has very quickly hoisted LNK Africa among the companies of choice.

Being already a supplier of goods and services in the field of IT, electronic security and surveillance, GPS Tracking, equipment rental, LNK Africa's vision is to provide quality service to its customers while remaining flexible and able to adapt to customer needs
